the mandela catalogue

The Mandela Catalogue is a popular analog horror web series about the fictional Mandela County, Wisconsin, being terrorized by demonic shapeshifters called "Alternates" that look like people but psychologically torture victims into suicide to steal their identities, often using old media like TVs and radios to spread their influence, with themes of religious corruption and psychological warfare. Presented as found footage and instructional tapes, the story unfolds as viewers piece together warnings about these beings, led by a false Archangel Gabriel, who exploit fear and humanity's weaknesses to invade
